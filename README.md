# HUNGRY GOOP

- A game about feeding a goop (slime).

The goop is YOURS! :TaePog:<br>
Track your IRL meals ! (Yes, you have to eat REAL LIFE FOOD) :blueCookie:<br>
Goop gets fed and spits out resources based on *what you eat*. :bluejerksmirk:<br>


You craft a base. The base collects extra resources.  :ONG:<br>
THEN! You use resources to set up defenses. A glooming threat is on its way (if you want it to be). :starewaiting:<br>
You can choose to be attacked by enemies (don't know what kind yet but yeah)<br>
The harder difficulty you pick, the more you stand to gain. If you lose, you don't lose anything, you just don't gain anything either.<br>
IF YOU WIN YOU GET COSMETICS AND SHIT!  :tf2_dear_god: <br>
Cosmetics which you can put on your goop! :tf2_soldier_conga: <br>
Not just cosmetics though. You can get goop shapes, wigs, eyes, random objects to stick inside or on your goop, paints, dyes, modeling tools :scream:, and much more !<br>
THEN you fight your friends in a battle of mimicry! :pregnant_man: <br>
You outfit and shape your goop to match a randomly picked person, character, or object. AND WHOEVER WINS...... is like cool and stuff so yeah that's the game. :proud: <br>

Think macro and calorie tracker X tower defense X idle game X Mii Maker.<br>
